My Work


Quarantine Companion


It’s an app that allows users to track healthy habits that they may have forgotten about during quarantine. Users input their data for habits such as socializing, exercise, sleep, and amount of freetime. The user can also go into the app and see their insights for their habits. The app also offers reminders for example if the user does not spend a healthy amount of time socializing they would get a notification like” hi, socializing is good for your mental health, why don’t you try texting someone?”. Through this app we are hoping people will attempt to form healthy habits by tracking their activity.
